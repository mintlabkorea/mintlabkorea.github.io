About

Daekyum Kim develops intelligent algorithms capable of understanding various aspects of human states — such as body kinematics, kinetics, activities, and intentions. His research encompasses machine learning, computer vision, robotics, and digital healthcare, with applications toward beneficial robotic and healthcare systems.

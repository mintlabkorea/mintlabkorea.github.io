Robot Intelligence
A hallmark of robotic intelligence is the ability to learn from perception. Vision based learning allows robots to contextualize beyond large language models by leveraging multimodal data in the form of Visual Language Models (VLMs). We aim to integrate visual input for human-robot interactions (HRI) such that our robots can truly experience the real world.

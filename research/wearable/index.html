Wearable Intelligence
Wearable sensors perform an important role in our daily lives through their assistance with human motion. These sensors are useful, providing real-time, continuous data streams of physiological signals. These signals are in turn used to interpret and predict user intentions. These intentions provide contextual intelligence for assistive robots that augment physiological movement for human rehabilitation or even enhance human mobility.
